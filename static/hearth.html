1) What we do

We select the companies we’re most excited about and where we can uniquely deliver value. We start with a low‑committal 8‑week project. If there’s mutual interest, it transitions into a two‑year partnership where we invest £25k–£50k and take an advisory and/or hands‑on role for 0.5%–1% in exchange for a 4–8 hr/week commitment.

5) What we look for

  • Edge‑fit: our skills directly address your current bottleneck.
  • Measurability: clean baselines so we can show impact in ≤ 8 weeks.
  • Founder cadence: coachable, decisive, weekly iteration.
  • EIS/SEIS ready: advance assurance or clean recent EIS; ordinary shares.
  • Unit economics: credible path to payback and margin.
  • Access & owner: analytics + technical access; a product/eng owner our side can interface with.

6) Selection pipeline

We don’t run an open application process — we proactively reach out to companies we select. We partner with at most three companies concurrently to stay hands‑on.

  2. Initial call ~20
  3. 8‑week project ~5
